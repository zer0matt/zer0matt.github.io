Introduction

Hey!
My name is Rosario Matteo Grammatico, I'm currently employed as a SOC Analyst at Iliad, in my free time I'm a security researcher who loves diving into low-level code and exploring how systems really work under the hood.
I spend most of my time breaking things to understand them — and making them more secure in the process.

I hope you find this blog interesting and maybe learn something new along the way.

I do security research as a hobby. My main interests include:

  • Malware Analysis
  • Windows Internals
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Binary Analysis
  • Vulnerability Research
  • EDR Evasion
  • Kernel Exploitation (ring 0)

Education

I earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Camerino.

Currently pursuing a Master's degree in Cyber Security at the University of Milan.
